Software Engineering Course

Software Engineering is a discipline concerned with the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software systems.

Course Overview

This course prepares students to become professional software developers. Students gain knowledge in programming, databases, operating systems, computer networks and web technologies.

The duration of study is four (4) years and graduates are awarded a Bachelor of Science degree.

Course Objectives

The main objective of this course is to equip students with technical and problem-solving skills.

Students will be able to design efficient software systems and apply software engineering principles correctly.

Department Message

Welcome to the Software Engineering Department.

We emphasize practical learning, innovation, and teamwork.

Special Information

The course requires strong mathematical skills.
Students must score at least C+ in KCSE Mathematics.
