Admission Requirements

To ensure student success in our intensive technical programs, all applicants must meet the following minimum criteria:

Academic Requirements

  • KCSE Mean Grade: C- (Minus) or equivalent
  • Mathematics: Minimum of C+
  • English: Minimum of C+

Documents Required

  • Copy of National ID or Passport
  • KCSE Certificate or result slip
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Basic computer literacy (typing and file management)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience to enroll?

No prior experience is required for any of our courses. We start from the very basics and build up progressively. All you need is a willingness to learn.

Are classes conducted online or in-person?

Classes are primarily in-person at our Kiharu campus. This ensures you have full access to our computer labs and can interact directly with instructors.

Can I pay tuition in installments?

Yes. We offer flexible payment plans. You can pay in monthly installments throughout the duration of your course. Contact our admissions office for details.

What happens after I complete my course?

We guarantee 100% internship placement for all graduates. Our career team works closely with partner companies to match you with relevant opportunities based on your skills and interests.

When does the next intake begin?

We run intakes in January, May, and September each year. Fill in the registration form below or contact us directly to reserve your spot in the next cohort.
